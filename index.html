ENGINEERING RESEARCH

Research & Innovation Focus

Driving the future of mechanical engineering through sustainable research and data-driven design.

Decentralized Wastewater Treatment

Design and optimization of scalable, high-efficiency sanitation solutions (bio-digesters, modular STPs, RO-integrated systems) for off-grid and rapidly urbanizing environments, with emphasis on performance under variable loading, energy constraints, and limited operational capacity.

Performance Intelligence & System Optimization

Data-driven monitoring and evaluation using advanced analytics, CFD-informed design, and AI-assisted modelling to assess treatment efficiency (BOD, COD, TSS), predict system behaviour, detect anomalies, and support proactive, real-time operational decisions.

AI, Hydroinformatics & Digital Water Systems

Application of machine learning, predictive modelling, and digital twin concepts to enhance reliability, automate control, and optimize lifecycle performance—integrating field data with computational models for simulation-to-implementation validation.

Climate-Resilient & Low-Energy Infrastructure

Development of robust, energy-efficient systems designed for flood-prone and power-constrained environments, incorporating resilience modelling, adaptive design strategies, and sustainable process optimization for long-term infrastructure performance.
